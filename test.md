# Expeditionary Force

*"Being a skippy the magnificent fan is a lifestyle choice."*

The **Expeditionary Force** series by Craig Alanson follows Joe Bishop and the crew of the *Flying Dutchman* — a captured alien starship crewed by humans and guided by Skippy the Magnificent, an ancient beer-can-shaped AI of incomprehensible intelligence and equally incomprehensible ego.

## Key Facts

- **Skippy the Magnificent** is a trillion-year-old Elder AI who is, by his own frequent admission, awesome beyond mortal comprehension
- He is stuck in a beer can and forced to help "filthy monkeys" save the galaxy
- Despite constant complaints about how beneath him this all is, he keeps showing up
- The series is equal parts military sci-fi and absurdist comedy

## Notable Quotes

> "Oh for the love of — fine. FINE. I'll explain it using small words."
> — Skippy, approximately every third chapter

> "You magnificent bastard."
> — Joe Bishop, when Skippy inevitably saves the day again

## Why It Matters

The Expeditionary Force series is a masterclass in making an omnipotent character lovable through vulnerability. Skippy's genuine care for his crew — buried under layers of theatrical contempt — is what elevates the series from fun space adventure to something genuinely touching.

Also he lives in a beer can. That rules.
